RailsBridge Guidelines are

  • First, do no harm. Then, help where you can.
  • Bridge the gap from aspiring developer to contributing community member, through mentoring, teaching, and writing.
  • Reach out to individuals and groups who are underrepresented in the community.
  • Collaborate with other groups with similar goals.

Get Involved

To succeed, we need your help! Here is what you can do to be a part of the RailsBridge vision.

  • Join the Google Group
  • Hang with us at the #railsbridge IRC channel on Freenode.
  • Help out with a current project
  • Propose new projects
  • Follow us on Twitter
  • Work to create the community you want.

Rails Tutor

This site is intended to be a repository for absolute beginner Rails courseware. Our goal is to help those with interest in programming but with no particular education or training in software development become contributing members of the Ruby on Rails community.

Ruby Challenge

Ruby Challenge is a system of puzzles to learn Ruby the fun way, by solving riddles from different categories - starting from the basics (Strings, Arrays), all the way up to advanced concepts like web stuff, metaprogramming etc. Can you hack your way through the maze of riddles?
